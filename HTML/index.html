FAQs

What is the nature of the Site?

This is a platform intending to offer students part-time jobs and investments that can be done offline in your own campus. All those in search of part-time job opportunities can utilize their leisure time to earn at ease.

How to join?

The procedure to join is very simple. You need to sign in which is absolutely free. Then you’ve to choose a job and a time slot which suits you .Afterward, you’ll be hired and paid according to your job module.

Is it necessary for me to have prior experience in order to join?

No ,the job will be either non-technical or less technical which makes it easy and available for all with or without experience.
